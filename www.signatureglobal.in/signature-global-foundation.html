Welcome to Signature Global Foundation

Empowering Communities, Building Futures

Signature Global Foundation Trust, the humanitarian arm of Signature Global (India) Limited, is committed to driving positive change and empowerment in society. Through our dedicated efforts, we strive to create a meaningful impact on the lives of the underprivileged, fostering a brighter future for all. Established under the esteemed provisions of the Indian Trust Act 1882 and under the vigilant guardianship of Mr. Pradeep Kumar Aggarwal, Chairman and Whole-time Director, our foundation focuses on a range of key areas to uplift our society.

Joining Hands for Goodness

At Signature Global Foundation Trust, we firmly believe that by working together, we can create a better world. Our initiatives are not only about monetary contributions but also about hands-on involvement. We actively engage in setting up healthcare camps, organizing mass marriages in underserved areas, and participating in initiatives that promote cleanliness and environmental sustainability.

Our Focus Areas

At Signature Global Foundation Trust, we are unwavering in our commitment to catalyze change in various spheres:

Education

We firmly believe that education is the cornerstone of progress. While free and compulsory education is a right for every child aged 6-14 in India, we understand that numerous challenges still persist, hindering many from accessing quality education. We step in to bridge the gap by supporting underprivileged children's education, from providing essentials like uniforms and study materials to identifying and rewarding meritorious students. We believe that by nurturing young minds, we are building a stronger foundation for society's future.

Environment

Our planet's well-being is essential for the sustenance of all life. Signature Global Foundation Trust actively participates in initiatives that promote a green and clean environment. From supporting the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan by constructing public toilets to contributing to clean water availability through the construction of water tanks, we understand the value of a healthy environment for the overall welfare of our society.

Healthcare

Ensuring access to healthcare is a fundamental right. We are committed to making quality healthcare accessible to all. Through healthcare camps, mass marriages in underserved areas, and participation in Jal Sewa initiatives, we endeavor to address the healthcare needs of marginalized communities, striving for the well-being of all.

Social Infrastructure

We understand the importance of robust social infrastructure for the development of any society. Our initiatives aim to bolster the social fabric by supporting projects that enhance the quality of life for all members of the community.
