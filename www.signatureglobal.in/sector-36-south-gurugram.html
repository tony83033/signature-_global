WELCOME TO

The Serenas

Nestled amidst the foothills of the Aravallis, this is a beautifully planned affordable residential project under Haryana Affordable Housing Policy. It is indeed a paradise for nature lovers. The picturesque view from the project will mesmerize the dwellers residing here. Spread in across 9.77 acres of land parcel, The Serenas is an abode surrounded with lush green environment & soothing neighbourhood. The project has a total of 1304 well-structured 2 BHK units, spacious enough to start a new settled life.

In order to step forward towards Value Housing, Signature Global pioneered and brought Padma Bhushan Architect Hafeez Contractor into affordable housing through 'The Serenas'. This HRERA approved project also offers modern & useful amenities that will certainly ease the life of the inhabitants here. These include basketball court, jogging track, open green area, kids' play area, amphitheater, theme gardens, skating rink & activities courts. Located in Sohna, South of Gurugram, this Signature Global project is bang on Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC). The connectivity from this project is superb. It is at the nearest approach of the first mall of Sohna – Signature Global Infinity Mall.

The Serenas is well-connected to the major hot spots of Gurugram. These include Golf Course Extension Road, Rajiv Chowk, Subhash Chowk & NH 8. The newly launched six-lane wide Sohna Elevated Road is also in close proximity to The Serenas. Going to Faridabad via Golf Course Extension Road is also convenient from the project. Sector 36, South of Gurugram is a rapid development residential zone hence, the neighbourhood of The Serenas is undoubtedly an organised region where one can find top-notch schools, hospitals, corporate parks, mall & other recreational centers.

Along with these features & location advantages, the project also possesses a uniquely structured high-street retail hub – Signature Global Signum 36. It is a hub where all the needs of the dwellers can be easily fulfilled.
