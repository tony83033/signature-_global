WELCOME TO

Signature Global Proxima I

Signature Global is India’s No.1 Affordable Housing Company*. Our mission is to provide “Har Parivar Ek Ghar”, because we believe in the philosophy “Kyuki Apna Ghar Toh Apna Hi Hota Hai.” With an endeavour to define the current conventions of Indian real estate development, we are championing excellence in craftsmanship, planning and service. So, we are certified in ISO 9001:2015; 14001:2015; and 45001:2018, delivering the best quality before the promised time. What’s more, we have introduced the highly advanced global green building certification IGBC. which provides a solution to capitalize on the value of green buildings by promoting benefits to the customers while protecting the environment.

The company has successfully launched 18 Affordable Housing projects, all in prime locations which include Gurugram, Sohna and Karnal in Haryana. We have successfully delivered Solera and Synera in Gurugram and offered the possession of Sunrise in Karnal, months before the expected time of delivery. Our success story also includes a commercial mall, focusing on the interests of customers in Vaishali, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.

Surrender process

In case of surrender of flat by a successful applicant, following amount shall be forfeited in addition to Rs. 25,000/- along with applicable taxes/charges/fee etc

Particular Amount to be Forfeited
In case of surrender/cancellation before the commencement of project Nil
Upto 1 year from the date of commencement of project 1% of the cost of flat
Upto 2 years from the date of commencement of project 3% of the cost of flat
After 2 years from the date of commencement of project 5% of the cost of flat

Such flats, (after surrender/cancellation) would be allotted in terms of Haryana Affordable Housing Policy, 2013 as amended up-to-date.

Penalty Clause

Any default in payment shall invite penal interest as provided in Rule 15 of the Haryana Real Estate Regulatory Authority, Rules 2017.
